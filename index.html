Welcome

Are you interested in cryptography and cybersecurity?

If you are, this site is for you!

Here you will decode cipher texts and hopefully learn something about cryptography

You will have helpful tools to help you to discover encoding algorithms and to decode it

The tools you will use are

  • Binary encoder/decoder: you will work with a lot of binary data
  • HEX: sometimes you will have to look at hex codes
  • ASCII Codes: looking at the ASCII codes will sometimes helps you
